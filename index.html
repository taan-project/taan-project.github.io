Why is travelling at night so dangerous?

Walking alone at night can present many dangers that could harm you in may ways. Some of these can include robbery, sexual harrasment, physical assault, kidnapping and more. If you consider to travel alone at night, please be more cautious of your surroundings and keep a way of communication. There are ways you can adapt if you travel alone at night. Travel with vehicles, a friend or stay in populated areas as the chance of risk can be higher. Overall please be very carefull when you travel at night.

The Negatives of Travelling Alone at Night.

- Getting Kidnapped
- Getting Sexually Harrased
- Getting Mugged
- Low Visibility
- Lost of Navigation
- Getting Hit by a vehicle
- Putting Yourself in Potential Danger
- Getting Followed Home
- Getting Intoxicated

Organisations you can call.

There are many organisations that are available 24/7.

These include:

- Emergency Services - 000
- Kids Helpline - 1800 55 1800
- Crime Stoppers - 1800 333 000
